Japan (like all other great countries) has undergone many trials and tribulations to get to where it is today. Below, is a timeline of how Japan came to be today.

- Jomon Period

Japan's history dates back approximately 38-40,000 years ago (10,000 BCE- 300 CE), with the Jomon period. They lived as Hunter-Gatherers, which were people who hunted for food using natural resources, doing hunting or fishing. They were equipped with bone and stone tools, like knives, axes, and grinding rocks.
